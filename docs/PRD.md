# Product Requirements Document — ASalvoCreative AI

ASalvoCreative AI helps people who haven't seriously used AI yet become fluent in it. This document defines what "launch-ready" means for asalvocreative.com and its supporting public repository. Each criterion is concrete and testable so progress is never ambiguous.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### 1. Clear value proposition on first visit
A visitor landing on asalvocreative.com can answer "what is this and who is it for" within five seconds. The hero line, subhead, and opening copy make the purpose obvious without requiring a scroll.

### 2. AI education corpus is sourced and substantive
docs/WHY-AI.md contains at least ten attributed quotes from named leaders across technology, finance, and creative industries, each with a verifiable source and date. The thesis section articulates a clear, original point of view — not just a collection of other people's words.

### 3. Founder credibility is established
The site includes a concise founder section that states who Andy Salvo is, what he has built (Founderos, Applied AI Club), and why he is qualified to teach AI fluency. No unsupported superlatives — just facts and links.

### 4. At least one free educational resource is publicly available
The site offers at least one piece of original educational content (a guide, walkthrough, or structured lesson) that a visitor can consume immediately at no cost. This proves ASalvoCreative AI delivers on its promise, not just talks about it.

### 5. Repository is clean and intentional
The public GitHub repo contains no dead files, no placeholder text, no TODO comments, and no empty directories. Every file that exists has a clear reason to exist. README.md accurately describes what is in the repo and what the current state is.

### 6. Site is functional on mobile and desktop
asalvocreative.com loads correctly and is fully usable on Chrome, Safari, and Firefox across both mobile and desktop viewports. No broken layouts, missing images, or dead links.

### 7. Contact or engagement path exists
A visitor who wants to learn more, work with us, or ask a question has a clear way to do so — whether that is an email link, a form, or a calendar booking page. The path is reachable within one click from any page.

### 8. No draft or speculative content is public
Every sentence on the site and in the repo is something Andy would stand behind if asked about it in an interview. Nothing is marked "coming soon" without a concrete plan behind it. The repo's public-facing policy (stated in CLAUDE.md and README.md) is fully enforced.

### 9. Applied AI Club presence is visible
The site references the Applied AI Club at Penn State with enough context that a visitor understands what it is and that Andy founded it. If the AI Innovation Day presentation (March 19, 2026) has occurred, a summary or link to materials is included.

### 10. Page load and basic SEO are acceptable
asalvocreative.com loads in under three seconds on a standard connection. The page has a proper title tag, meta description, and Open Graph tags so that links shared on LinkedIn or in messages render with correct preview text and imagery.
